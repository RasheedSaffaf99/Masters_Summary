## 1. Key Concepts

### • Random Variables
A random variable \(X\) with PDF \(f(x)\) satisfies:

\[
\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x)\,dx = 1
\]

### • Exponential Distribution

\[
f(x|\lambda) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x}
\]

### • Poisson Process

\[
P(N(t)=k) = \frac{(\lambda t)^k e^{-\lambda t}}{k!}
\]
